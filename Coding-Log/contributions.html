How we use your Contributions! The money that you kindly donate is directly supporting the app. The money is collected into a pool where I may sometimes buy myself a coffee which will continue to make me inspired to work on the app. Also I will use the funds to directly support the app like eventually I want to convert it to cloud data(which cost money).

TL;DR your contributions are used to directly support the app and some amount is for our developers(including me)

Also contributors if so choose can enroll in a mailing list where I update them on what the money is being spent on and how much money was recieved that month/period. (email codinglog.help@gmail.com for details on becoming subscribed)
