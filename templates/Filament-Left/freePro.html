Free Pro

If you would like to have free pro in the future(it will cost money even if you already have downloaded the app) all you need to do is send an email to masonhorder.dev@gmail.com containing at least 30 images of spools of filament. Once it becomes paid we will get you set up on the pro plan. Thanks so much because you are helping make this a better product!






How to take good photos:

  • -ONLY ONE spool in frame
  • -Make sure the full spool is in frame, horizontally it should take up 1/2 the screen.
  • -Keep the spool straight on
  • -Not too much stuff in the background
