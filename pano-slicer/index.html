What is Pano Slicer?

Pano Slicer is the best tool for splicing wide photos or panoramics for posting on Instagram. When downloading you get the option to edit sizes, photo count and even crop the image. Pano Slicer is great for professional photographers or your average Instagram user. Download for free and up your Instagram posts game!
